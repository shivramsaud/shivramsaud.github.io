About

I am a final-year B.Tech AI student at Kathmandu University researching low-resource NLP for Nepali. My work covers machine translation, hate speech detection, semantic video retrieval, and vision-based IoT. I have a first-author publication at the CHiPSAL workshop (ACL Anthology), a co-authored paper in Springer LNEE, and an accepted shared task at EEUCA 2026. I received a NAST Research Grant (NPR 1,00,000) for IoT-based agriculture and mentor Raspberry Pi workshops at KU’s Robotics Club. I am seeking NLP research opportunities to contribute to language technologies for underserved communities.
