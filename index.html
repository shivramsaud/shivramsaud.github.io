About Me

I am an undergraduate AI researcher at Kathmandu University, driven by the challenge of building language technologies for Nepali and other low-resource languages. My work spans speech recognition, machine translation, hate speech detection, and vision-based IoT systems.

I have published at ACL workshops, received a NAST research grant for IoT-based agriculture, and mentored Raspberry Pi workshops. I am actively seeking NLP research opportunities where I can bridge academic studies with industrial practice.
