About

SRS

I am a final-year B.Tech AI student at Kathmandu University with a research focus on low-resource NLP for Nepali. My work spans machine translation, hate speech detection, semantic video retrieval, and vision-based IoT systems.

I have a first-author publication at the CHiPSAL workshop (ACL Anthology), a co-authored paper in Springer LNEE on IoT-integrated number plate recognition, and an accepted shared task on gaming toxicity detection at EEUCA 2026. I was awarded a NAST Research Grant (NPR 1,00,000) for IoT-based smart mushroom cultivation.

Beyond research, I mentor Raspberry Pi workshops at Kathmandu University’s Robotics Club and work on applying low-cost digital tools in agriculture. I am actively seeking NLP research opportunities where I can contribute to language technologies for underserved communities.
